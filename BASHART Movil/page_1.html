We at BASHART Performance have tuned the diesel top model of the current BMW 3 Series-generation G20: the M340d. Our creation is called MH3 400d and offers a performance of 380 hp and 770 Nm.

As a powerful top model, the Cupra Formentor VZ5 served our team as the basis for our latest creation: the CP 500. We installed our proven BASHART MHtronik auxiliary control unit and a BASHART intercooler by Wagner. This results in a whopping 490 hp and up to 630 Nm of torque!

The BASHART MH4 GTR II has made its official debut, but our project is not based on just any M4. No, this GTR is based on the brand new lightweight M4 CSL, which is limited to 1,000 units. Our MH4 GTR II is limited to only 1.

FOCUS ON DETAIL

OPTIMISING POWER TO WEIGHT RATIO

At the heart of our wide range of tuning upgrades, both technical and visual, you will find the optimization of the power to weight ratio. This trademark of the BASHART company offers our international clients a constant high standard incorporated into the many comprehensive modifications which we develop in-house. We at BASHART offer, sell and install our parts on a daily basis and in great numbers across the globe via our partners and dealers.

IN-HOUSE DEVELOPMENT IS OUR SPECIALTY

One of BASHART’s core competences is the development, production and distribution of weight-optimized performance products. Our BASHART product portfolio ranges from in-house developed carbon fiber parts and high-grade exhaust systems to perfectly fitted wheel/tyre combinations. Upon request, we work with our international clients on creating unique projects, which offers both interior and exterior visual enhancements next to a wide range of technical and aerodynamic upgrades improving the vehicle’s performance drastically.
